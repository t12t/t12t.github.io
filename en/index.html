Hello accessibility!

T12t, means accessibility and it's the swedish equivalent to a11y, a numeronym for accessibility. Our focus is digital accessibility. We want to be a place for people to meet and learn from one another.

Our meetups

Find us on meetup.com as t12t - Stockholm. Our goal is to arrange a couple of meetups every year. Read more about our meetups on our meetup page.

Our newsletter

In our newsletter you can find articles, tips and news regarding digital accessibility. More info and signup on our newsletter page.

Our Slack

Our Slack is open to anyone interested. We hope it works as a good way to meet digitally.

Safety and inclusion

We have a Code of Conduct that we expect everyone to follow. By taking part in our community, whether that is on a physical meetup, a discussion on Slack or elsewhere, you are required to agree with our Code of Conduct.
